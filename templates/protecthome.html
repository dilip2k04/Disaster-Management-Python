Earthquake Protection Tips

Structural Safety

Secure Heavy Furniture

Anchor bookcases, cabinets, and appliances to wall studs

Brace Overhead Fixtures

Secure light fixtures, ceiling fans, and other hanging items

Install Latches on Cabinets

Prevent contents from spilling out during shaking

Emergency Preparedness

Identify Safe Spots

Know where to take cover in each room (under sturdy furniture, against interior walls)

Practice Drills

Conduct regular earthquake drills with all household members

Essential Home Protection Kit

Every home should have these basic emergency supplies:

Fire extinguisher (ABC type)
Emergency water supply (1 gallon per person per day)
First aid kit
Flashlights with extra batteries
Utility shut-off tool
Emergency radio (battery or hand-crank)
Non-perishable food (3-day supply)
Heavy-duty gloves
